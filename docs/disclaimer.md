# Scientific and educational disclaimer

Restless Pacific is an educational and portfolio project. It is not an alert,
forecasting, early-warning, hazard-assessment, evacuation, navigation, or
emergency-response service.

Earthquake feeds can be revised or deleted after automatic publication.
Volcano and tsunami records contain historical reporting gaps, changing
classifications, approximate locations, and partial dates. An absent record
does not establish that an event did not happen. A nearby plate boundary is
derived spatial context and is not, by itself, causal attribution. Counts and
trends—especially before modern instrumental coverage—must not be interpreted
as a complete measure of changing global activity.

The “Ring of Fire” is a popular geographic description, not one continuous
scientific structure with a defined start and end. The journey begins in New
Zealand and travels clockwise solely as an editorial convention. Membership is
shown according to a named, versioned source definition.

For current conditions and safety instructions, use official national and
local authorities, volcano observatories, tsunami warning centers, and the
United States Geological Survey or relevant equivalent. Do not make safety or
travel decisions from this application.
